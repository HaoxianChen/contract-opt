\section{Selective View Materialization}
\label{sec-materialization}

This section first defines the imperative control flow of smart contract execution and optimizes it through view elimination based on
arithmetic expression simplification.
Next, it establishes the validity and minimal form of a materialization plan 
based on the optimized imperative control flow. 
It then introduces an algorithm that systematically identifies 
all minimal valid materialization plans and determines the most gas-efficient option through profiling.
